Questions You May Ask

Frequently Asked Questions

What if I don't get results?+

Our approach has helped scale multiple brands and creators across different niches — so it's very unlikely that what's worked for others won't work for you too.

How are you different from a freelance editor?+

Instead of one person juggling everything, you get a system — strategy, editing, colour, and delivery — designed around consistent output, not just a single video at a time.

What services do you offer?+

Short-form, YouTube, podcast editing, ad creatives, motion graphics and brand films — plus SEO, Meta & Google Ads if you need the full package.

Can you handle bulk or long-term projects?+

Yes — whether it's 5 videos a month or 50, we have the workflow to manage ongoing content without you needing to chase us for updates.

What's your turnaround time?+

Short-form edits typically turn around in 24-48 hours. Long-form and brand films depend on scope — we'll give you a clear timeline before starting.
